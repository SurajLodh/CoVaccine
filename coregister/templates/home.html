Morden Way to Register Covid-19 vaccination


Book Online Appointment

There’s no app for Covid-19 vaccine. You would need a PC with working internet or use any internet browser on your mobile like Google Chrome or Safari. You would need a working mobile number and photo ID proof like Aadhaar card, Driving License, etc.

Register

How to Register

You can register for Covid-19 vaccine by logging on “www.CoVacine.com”’. Enter your mobile number to receive OTP for verification via SMS. Click on the ‘Verify’ button to complete the registration process.

Register

Confirm Vaccine Registration on CoVaccine website

After registering on the CoWIN website, a new ‘Registration of Vaccination’ page will appear. Enter details like Photo ID type, Photo ID Proof number, name, date of birth, gender and other details. Then click on the Register button. You will receive necessary details regarding vaccination via an SMS. Check the details properly. You will get a Beneficiary Reference ID at the time of registering for the vaccine, save this ID.
